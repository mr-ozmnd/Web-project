About Our Resort

Located near the Bentota Lake, Dalmanuta Resort offers world-class amenities, gourmet dining, and breathtaking views for an unforgettable experience.
Nestled amidst the serene beauty of Bentota in Sri Lanka, Dalmanuta Gardens is a charming 3.5-star hotel that offers a peaceful retreat for travelers seeking relaxation and rejuvenation.
With its lush green gardens, tranquil atmosphere, and warm hospitality, this hotel is the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.
